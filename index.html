About the Competition

Hosted by the University Law Society, the Annual National Moot Court Competition stands as our flagship academic advocacy tournament. Designed to test structural reasoning, clarity of thought, and high-pressure legal interpretation, this year’s case problem challenges contingents to navigate unprecedented intersections of Constitutional Law and emerging technological landscapes.

Participants will present their arguments before panels of distinguished legal practitioners, academic jurists, and sitting judges. Beyond the courtroom simulation, the event provides a critical networking arena for students looking to benchmark their legal research skills against elite peers nationally.
